commands:
GiveActorsItems
- attempts to give items to an actor (starts with the first actor)
  any leftover items will be given to actor next in line
  if there are still leftover items after all actors then the
  leftovers will be placed in the bag
GiveActorsItemsSilent
- same as above but without messages
